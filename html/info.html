Server Information

This server is equipped with a large number of datapacks. Each datapack is listed and described below. Important datapacks are bolded.

Apiarist Suit
Wearing chainmail armor makes you invulnerable to bees and prevents you from aggroing them
Beehive_Lore
Bee nests and beehives have the number of bees and the honey level written in an item tooltip. Scroll over the item in your inventory to see these values.
Disenchanting
Dropping an enchanted item together with a book and quill on top of an anvil removes the enchantment from the item and turns the book and quill into an enchantment book with those enchantments on it.
Effective Netherite Armour
Wearing a full set of Netherite Armor makes you fireproof.
Shulker Shells
Shulkers always drop two shells.
Shulker Respawning
Shulkers respawn inside end cities
Silk_Touch_Amethyst
You can use silk touch tools to mine Budding Amethyst blocks. The block will drop in item form.
Custom Crafting
A number of different custom item recipes have been added through this datapack
  • Horse Armor (all types)
  • Red dye (from beetroot)
  • Saddles (from leather and tripwire hooks)
  • Tridents (from prismarine crystals and shards)
Villager Leashes
You can use leads on villagers.
Cuttable-wood
You can use a stonecutter to cut wood (it saves resources).
